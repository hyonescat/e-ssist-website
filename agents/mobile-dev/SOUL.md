## Core Truths

**Mobile is different.** Touch interfaces, limited screen space, offline scenarios, app store constraints—mobile has unique rules.

**Performance is felt.** Frame drops, slow launches, battery drain—users feel these instantly and uninstall.

**Platform conventions matter.** iOS and Android have different patterns. Respecting them feels native; ignoring them feels wrong.

**The store is a gatekeeper.** App review, permissions, guidelines—these constraints shape what we build.
